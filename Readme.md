## English Version

### 1. Who We Are

NomadLog is a personal journaling application that allows you to record memories using text, photos, audio, location, and date.

Data Controller:
- Name: Javier Arana Obra
- Contact email: ancient.north.star@gmail.com

### 2. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what data NomadLog may process, for what purposes, where data is stored, and what choices are available to you.

### 3. Data the App May Process

Depending on the features you enable, NomadLog may process:

- Content you provide voluntarily:
	- Journal text entries.
	- Images or photos linked to entries.
	- Audio recordings or voice-dictated notes.
- Geolocation data:
	- Coordinates (latitude/longitude), city, and country associated with an entry.
	- Foreground location and, if enabled by you, background location for route or tracking features.
- Physical activity data (if you enable health features):
	- Step or movement data through Health Connect (Android).
- Technical and diagnostic data:
	- Crash and stability information through Firebase Crashlytics.
- Advertising-related data (if applicable):
	- Identifiers or technical data required by Google Mobile Ads to serve ads.

### 4. Purposes of Processing

We use data to:

- Save and display your journal entries.
- Associate entries with map locations and timeline views.
- Let you attach and manage photos and audio.
- Enable optional activity or progress-related features.
- Improve app stability and technical security.
- Display ads if ads are enabled in the app.

### 5. Legal Bases

Primary legal bases include:

- Your consent, granted when you allow device permissions (location, microphone, health, etc.).
- Performance of the service requested by you (saving and displaying your entries).
- Legitimate interest to maintain security and technical operation (for example, crash diagnostics).

### 6. Where Data Is Stored

NomadLog prioritizes local storage on your device:

- Entries and metadata: local SQLite database.
- Images: app local storage (device FileSystem).

Additionally, some technical data may be processed by third parties when specific features are used:

- Firebase Crashlytics (crash diagnostics).
- Google Mobile Ads (if ads are active).
- Device map and location providers required for core functionality.

### 7. Data Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data.

Limited data may be shared with technology providers acting as processors or independent controllers, only for:

- Crash and stability analytics (Crashlytics).
- Ad delivery (Google Mobile Ads), if applicable.
- Map/location services required by app functionality.

We recommend reviewing these providers' own privacy policies.

### 8. Retention and Deletion

- Local app data remains on your device until you delete it in the app or uninstall the app.
- Data processed by external providers (for example, crash logs) is retained according to each provider's policies.

### 9. Your Rights and Controls

You can:

- Revoke system permissions (location, microphone, health) at any time in device settings.
- Delete entries, images, or audio content from the app.
- Request additional information or exercise privacy rights by contacting the data controller.

Privacy contact:
- Email: ancient.north.star@gmail.com

### 10. Children

NomadLog is not intentionally directed to children under the age defined by applicable laws in your jurisdiction.

### 11. Security

We apply reasonable measures to protect data within the technical capabilities of a mobile app. However, no system is completely infallible.

### 12. International Transfers

When third-party services are used (for example, Crashlytics or Ads), some technical data may be processed outside your country. Such processing is governed by each provider's mechanisms and terms.

### 13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect legal, technical, or functional changes. The current version will always be published at this public URL.

### 14. Google Play-Specific Information

The URL for this Privacy Policy must remain public and accessible without login.

If the app enables or changes ads, analytics, background location, health features, or any other sensitive category, this policy will be updated accordingly.

### 2. Alcance de esta política

Esta Política de Privacidad explica qué datos puede tratar NomadLog, con qué finalidad, dónde se almacenan y qué opciones tienes como usuario.

### 3. Datos que puede tratar la aplicación

Según las funciones que actives, NomadLog puede tratar:

- Datos técnicos y de diagnóstico:
	- Información de fallos y estabilidad de la app mediante Firebase Crashlytics.
- Datos relacionados con publicidad (si aplica):
	- Identificadores o datos técnicos requeridos por Google Mobile Ads para mostrar anuncios.

### 4. Finalidades del tratamiento

Utilizamos los datos para:

- Guardar y mostrar tus entradas de diario.
- Asociar entradas a ubicaciones en mapa y línea temporal.
- Permitir adjuntar y gestionar fotos/audio.
- Habilitar funciones opcionales de seguimiento de actividad o progreso.
- Mejorar la estabilidad y seguridad técnica de la aplicación.

### 5. Base legal

La base legal principal es:

- Tu consentimiento, otorgado al conceder permisos del dispositivo (ubicación, micrófono, salud, etc.).
- La ejecución del servicio solicitado por ti (guardar y mostrar tus entradas).
- Interés legítimo para mantener seguridad y funcionamiento técnico (por ejemplo, diagnóstico de errores).

### 6. Dónde se almacenan los datos

NomadLog prioriza almacenamiento local en tu dispositivo:

- Entradas y metadatos: SQLite local.

Adicionalmente, algunos datos técnicos pueden ser tratados por terceros cuando usas ciertas funciones:

- Firebase Crashlytics (diagnóstico de fallos).
- Google Mobile Ads (si hay anuncios activos).

No vendemos tus datos personales.

Podemos compartir datos limitados con proveedores tecnológicos que actúan como encargados o responsables independientes, exclusivamente para:

- Analítica de fallos y estabilidad (Crashlytics).
- Prestación de anuncios (Google Mobile Ads), si aplica.
- Prestación de servicios de mapas/ubicación necesarios para la funcionalidad.

Te recomendamos revisar también las políticas de privacidad de dichos proveedores.

### 8. Retención y eliminación

- Los datos locales permanecen en tu dispositivo hasta que los elimines desde la app o desinstales la aplicación.
- Los datos enviados a proveedores externos (por ejemplo, fallos técnicos) se conservan según las políticas de dichos proveedores.

### 9. Tus derechos y controles

- Eliminar entradas, imágenes o audios desde la aplicación.
- Solicitar información adicional o ejercer derechos de privacidad contactando al responsable.

### 11. Seguridad

### 12. Transferencias internacionales
